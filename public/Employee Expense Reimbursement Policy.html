Employee Expense Reimbursement Policy

Objective:

This Employee Expense Reimbursement Policy outlines the procedures and guidelines for the efficient and equitable reimbursement of business-related expenses incurred by employees.. The policy aims to provide a transparent process for expense reimbursement.

Scope:

This policy applies to all employees of IOT Monks Private Limited who are authorized to incur business-related expenses. It encompasses various categories of expenditures, including but not limited to travel, meals, accommodation, and other business-related disbursements.

Expense Categories:

1. Travel Expenses:

  • Employees are required to submit detailed receipts for airfare, lodging, and    transportation.
  • Per day rates for meals and incidental expenses adhere to the company's travel policy.

2. Meals and Entertainment:

  • Original itemized receipts are mandatory for reimbursement.
  • Alcohol expenses must be reasonable and directly associated with business activities.

3. Accommodation:

  • Employees must provide detailed hotel invoices with a clear zero balance.

4. Miscellaneous Expenses:

  • All other business-related expenses necessitate supported receipts and a concise explanation of purpose.

Reimbursement Process:

1. Submission of Expenses:

  • Employees are obligated to submit the nature of the expense along with the receipt/invoice within 15 days of incurring the expense in the ESS portal.
  • All receipts must be meticulously attached, clearly indicating date, amount, and purpose.

2. Approval:

  • Expense reports undergo thorough review and approval by the employee's immediate supervisor or department head.
  • The approval process is expedited within 5 business days.

3. Processing:

  • The Finance Department expeditiously processes approved reimbursements within 15 business days.
  • Reimbursements are disbursed through direct deposit or check, based on the employee's preference.

Policy Compliance:

1. Receipts:

  • Incomplete or illegible receipts may result in delayed or declined reimbursement.
  • Employees are responsible for retaining legible copies of receipts for a minimum of six months post-reimbursement.

2. Authorized Expenses:

  • Reimbursement is exclusively provided for necessary, reasonable, and directly business-related expenses.

3. Policy Violations:

  • Non-compliance with this policy may lead to the denial of reimbursement or appropriate disciplinary measures.

Contact Information:

For inquiries or clarification on the reimbursement process, employees may contact the Finance Department.
