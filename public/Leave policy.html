Unauthroised/Uninformed Absence

Any absence from work without prior authorization or notification will be considered as "Absent" and not classified as leave.

Leave Intimation

Leave requests must be communicated to the reporting manager at least one week in advance, unless in cases of emergencies or sickness.

Leave Approval

All employees at IOT Monks Private Limited are required to apply their leave requests through ESS Portal to their reporting manager. The reporting manager holds the authority to grant leave.

Earned Leave

  • An employee accrues one earned leave each month
  • An employee can accumulate a maximum EL balance of 45 days
  • EL cannot be clubbed with CL.

Sick Leave

  • Employees are entitled to sick leave to address personal illness or medical appointments.
  • The company grants 12 number of sick leave days per year sick leave days per calendar year to employees.
  • SL balance is credited every month to an employee’s account by 01st of every month.
  • To avail sick leave, employees must follow the designated reporting and documentation procedures as outlined by the company.

Casual Leave

  • Employees will be granted 12 casual leave days per year for personal reasons, short breaks, or unforeseen circumstances.
  • CL balance is credited every month to an employee’s account by 01st of every month.
  • Casual leave can be taken in full or half-day increments, as per the organization's policies and work requirements.

Leave Encashment

  • A maximum of 45 leaves can be accumulated during the employment tenure, for encashment.
  • Reimbursement for accrued leave exceeding 45 days will be processed at the end of the calendar year and credited in the next payment cycle.
  • No encashment of leave is permissible during the service and the accumulated leave can only be cashed at the time of full and final settlement, post separation of an employee through resignation or retirement.

Maternity Leave

No woman shall be entitled to maternity benefit unless she has actually worked with IOT Monks Private Limited for a period of not less than eighty days (80) in the twelve months immediately preceding the date of her expected delivery. The maximum period for which any woman shall be entitled to maternity benefit shall be twenty-six weeks (180 days) of which not more than 8 weeks can be availed prior to the delivery.

Paternity Leave

Male employees are entitled to paternity benefits if he has completed 12 months of service with the company. Paternity leave is eligible for Male Employees, for a period of 15 days to take care of wife and new born child. He can avail this leave 15 days before or within 6 months from the date of delivery of the child.

Bereavement Leave

IOT Monks Private Limited grants a 3-day paid leave of absence to employees in the event of the death of the employee’s current spouse, child, parent, legal guardian, brother, sister, grandparent, or grandchild.

Cancellation Of Leave

  • The reporting manager can also cancel the once sanctioned leave on situational / need basis. If the employee proceeds to avail the canceled leave then those days will be treated as absence from duty and the rules pertaining to absence from duty will be applied.

Leave Management

The ESS Portal (RazorPay)  will be used for the entire leave procedure.

  • Employees have to apply for the respective leave (CL / SL / EL) through ESS Portal (Razorpay).
  • CLand EL have to be applied through the ESS Portal and sent for approval to the Reporting Manager at least a week in advance.
  • Leave notification will be sent to their Reporting Manager for approval. Once approved, approval notification will be sent to the Employee.
  • All leaves should be applied by the Employee through the ESS Portal before proceeding on their leave. In case of emergency circumstances when leave cannot be applied in advance, telephonic/email intimation to the Reporting Manager and HR must be done and it should be regularized within 2 Days from resuming their duty in the ESS Portal.
  • Absence from work without notification will be considered as Loss of Pay (LOP).
  • Employees who have taken more than two consecutive sick leaves must submit a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner or doctor to the HR department.

Leave during probation/training period

During the probation/training period an employee is expected to spend adequate time to learn responsibilities. Any leave taken during the probation period will be considered as LWP( leave without pay) will extend the probation/ training period.

Leave during notice period

During the notice period an employee is expected to spend adequate time to hand over responsibilities to another assigned employee. Any leave taken during notice period will be treated as LWP(Leave without pay) and will extend the notice period.        

5.6 Compensatory Off

  1. An employee is eligible for compensatory time off when they have worked on a national holiday, festival, or a declared off day due to an important assignment.
  2. Assignments on such days, i.eNational holidays, festivals, or declared off days, must be initiated and approved by the reporting manager and communicated to the HR Department.
  3. Compensatory time off must be taken within 15 days; otherwise, it will expire.
  4. On the day an employee is taking compensatory time off, they must inform their immediate reporting manager. Once approved, it is the immediate manager's responsibility to inform HR and apply for it in the ESS portal.
