INTERNSHIP

1. The company offers an enriching internship program aimed at providing practical industry exposure in both manufacturing and corporate domains.

2. Candidates, who should be current students or recent graduates eligible to work in the internship's location, undergo a standard selection by HR, with the program typically lasting 2-6 months.

3. While interns receive mentorship, possible stipends, work-related expense reimbursements, and access to company facilities, they are not entitled to benefits reserved for full-time employees.

4. Interns are, however, expected to adhere to professional standards, including compliance with codes of conduct, safety protocols, and confidentiality agreements.

5. Internships do not guarantee subsequent employment, though performance evaluations conducted during the experience are influential in any potential hiring decisions post-internship.
