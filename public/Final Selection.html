FINAL SELECTION

Final selection is made upon acceptance of the selection committee’s recommendations. The selected candidate is offered the position subject to clearance of medical examination.

1. MEDICAL EXAMINATION:
For all the positions, the medical checkup can be performed by the doctor available at the premises/suggested by HR or the candidate can produce a fitness certificate from any Civil Hospital. The doctor preferred by the Management may ask the candidate to undergo further tests if required post cross checking the submitted reports.
