Employee Self-Service (ESS- Razorpay) Portal Usage Policy

Purpose:

The Employee Self-Service (ESS) Portal is an integral part of IOT Monks Private Limited commitment to providing employees with convenient access to essential HR and administrative functions. This policy outlines the guidelines and expectations for the usage of the ESS Portal to ensure consistency, security, and efficiency.

Access and Authorization:

  • Access to the ESS Portal is restricted to employees of IOT Monks Private Limited who have been granted proper authorization by the HR department.
  • Users are required to keep their login credentials confidential and must not share their username or password with others.
  • Any unauthorized access to the ESS Portal is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action.

Usage Guidelines:

  • Employees are encouraged to use the ESS Portal for routine HR transactions, such as updating personal information, accessing pay statements, and submitting leave requests.
  • Any information submitted through the ESS Portal must be accurate and up-to-date. Employees are responsible for ensuring the correctness of the data they input.
  • The ESS Portal should not be used for any unlawful or malicious activities. Any misuse of the portal will be subject to disciplinary measures, including termination.

Data Security:

  • Employees are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their personal and sensitive information within the ESS Portal.
  • In the event of a lost or compromised login credential, employees must immediately report it to the IT department to prevent unauthorized access.
  • IOT Monks Private Limited industry-standard security measures to protect the data within the ESS Portal. Any identified security concerns should be reported promptly.

System Availability:

  • The ESS Portal may undergo scheduled maintenance, during which access may be temporarily restricted. Employees will be notified in advance of any planned maintenance windows.
  • In the event of unexpected system outages, IOT Monks Private Limited will make reasonable efforts to restore service promptly. Employees may contact the IT helpdesk for assistance.

Compliance

  • Usage of the ESS Portal must comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and company’s policies.
  • Any concerns or questions regarding the ESS Portal should be directed to the HR department or the IT helpdesk.
