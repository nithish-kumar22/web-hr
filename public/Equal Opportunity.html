EQUAL OPPORTUNITY

 

1. Gourmet Popcornica is fully committed to maintaining a policy of equal employment and advancement opportunities, ensuring that all qualified individuals are treated without discrimination.

2. Regardless of race, colour, religion, sex, age, marital status, national origin, or handicap, every employee and applicant will receive fair and equal consideration in all aspects of employment. This policy is designed to promote a diverse and inclusive work environment.

3. Recruitment and promotion decisions are based solely on relevant factors such as attitude, skills, job performance, results generated, and alignment with the cultural requirements of the position. Managers and functional heads bear direct responsibility for implementing and upholding this policy within their departments. They are accountable for ensuring that all employees understand and adhere to this policy.

4. Gourmet Popcornica values open communication and encourages employees to bring forth any questions or concerns regarding workplace discrimination. Employees can freely report incidents or express concerns without fear of retaliation or reprisal. The company is dedicated to addressing such matters promptly and effectively.
