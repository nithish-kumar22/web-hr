Office Equipment & Usage Policy

Purpose

This policy is to control and prevent wastage by using the internal communication devices (internet & telephone) and office equipment (scanner, printer and photocopier). It aims to protect the information assets of the company.

Scope

It is to provide guidance on the circumstances and conditions for the usage of Internal Communication devices and Office Equipment.

Coverage

This policy applies to all employees who utilize the technical resources of the company.

● Telephone Calls

Telephones are intended for the use of serving our customers and in conducting the Company’s business. Personal usage during business hours is discouraged except for extreme emergencies.

● Internet Usage

Company provided systems, computer files, the e-mail system, Internet access and the software furnished to employees are company property and are to be used for company business only, and not for personal use. Employees may access the Internet for studying and updating knowledge to either complete their certifications or to retrieve the information that is useful for the individual growth and also for the growth of the company.

● Printer & Photocopier Usage

All the employees should understand the importance of using the office equipment economically.

Letterheads to be used by the authorized Managers only. Letter Heads are to be utilized for official purposes only and not to be left around work stations unattended. Also when not in use, Letter Heads to be kept under Lock and Key. Ensure there are no letter heads placed in the printer while taking a print if it is not required to take on the same.

All the letterheads are numbered and logged in the register.
