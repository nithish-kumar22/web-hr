Definitions

● Appointing Authority shall mean the CEO of the company or his delegate.

● Leadership shall mean the Management Committee Team who are key officials handpicked and empowered to decide the course of the Company.

● Company means IOT Monks Private Limited. It includes the CEO and other officials, who are empowered to exercise powers on behalf of the company.

● Corporate Office shall mean the Registered office of IOT Monks Private Limited situated at Coimbatore.

● Employees means a person employed by the Company and classified as permanent, probationer, temporary, casual, apprentice/trainee/trainer/learner and a fixed term employee.

● Continuous Service means uninterrupted service and includes service which is interrupted by sickness, accident, leave, lay off, strike or lockout or cessation of work not due to any fault of the employee concerned.

● Year shall mean a period of twelve months commencing on 1st of April of a particular year and ending on 31st March of the subsequent year.
