Probationary period

Any appointment made on IOT Monks Private Limited shall be subject to a probation period as specified in the appointment/employment letter. During this time, the employee will have the first opportunity to evaluate the company as a place to work, and we will have our first opportunity to evaluate the employee as an employee.

A probationary period of three(3) months for freshers from the date of joining will be followed and feedback from immediate performance review shall be conducted after this period.

When performance warrants it and at the discretion of management, the probation period may be extended.

Upon Satisfactory completion of the probation period, the employee receives an order of confirmation and will become a full time regular employee of the company.

All employees regardless of classification, status or length of service are expected to meet and maintain company standards for job performance (as per their defined KRA) and behavior (as mentioned in the Code of Conduct).
