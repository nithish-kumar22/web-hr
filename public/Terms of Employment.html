Terms of Employment

The Terms of the employment will be as per the details contained in the Appointment Letter. The age of a person at the time of his joining the service of the company shall not be less than 18 years. Every employee must declare on his/her appointment, his/her date of birth according to the Christian era and produce confirmatory evidence like photocopy of PAN, Aadhar, and certificates along with resume and 4 passport size photos. The employment contract is a contract between the individual employee and the company and the terms of contract are individual to each employee. Hence the employee is expected not to share the terms of the contract with others, including the fellow Employees and also sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement(NDA).
