Rejoining policy

The streamline and strengthen the joining of an employee again into the organization in an existing or new work site/location/department.

In the event of a rejoining process, the credibility and the validity of the candidature for rejoining is substantiated under the following clauses / instances:

The employee has resigned and been relieved from the organization with the proper  documentation.

The Conduct report of the employee as confirmed by the Group head or  Reporting Manager is deemed “Good” verbally or otherwise.

The Employee has sought consent to join again on the grounds of Illness or a family emergency whose extent of impact can be proved through the relevant medical documents or other written collaterals.

The employee will be considered as a new applicant and his previous candidature will not be considered credible for any stages of the joining process.
