CONFIDENTIALITY

 

1. The preservation and security of confidential business information and trade secrets are of utmost significance for the prosperity and sustainability of Gourmet Popcornica LLP. In order to uphold this commitment, it is mandatory for all employees to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement prior to commencing their employment with the organization.

2. Under no circumstances should employees engage in the improper utilisation or disclosure of trade secrets or confidential business information, regardless of whether they personally stand to gain from such actions.

3. Any violation of this policy will be met with rigorous disciplinary measures, including the possibility of termination of employment and the initiation of legal proceedings.
