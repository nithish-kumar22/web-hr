Confidentiality

The protection of confidential business information and trade secrets is vital to the interests and success of IOT Monks Private Limited. All employees from the cadre of Supervisors/Team Leaders are required to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement as a condition of employment.

Such confidential information includes, but is not limited to, the following examples:

  • Compensation data
  • Financial information
  • Marketing strategies
  • Pending projects and proposals
  • Personnel/Payroll records and
  • Conversations between any persons Employed with the company

Employees should not publish any article or statement, deliver any lecture or broadcast or make any communication to the press, including magazine publication relating to the Company's service / business or to any matter with which the Company may be concerned, unless they have previously applied to and obtained the written permission from the Company.
