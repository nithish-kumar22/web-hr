MANPOWER REQUISITION

1. Planning for manpower requirements is initiated by the concerned department by filling the MRF (Manpower Requisition Form). A proposal duly approved by the approved authority: Managing Partners, Department HODs wherever applicable has to be sent to HR. The MRF should capture all the details such as pay level, tenure of employment etc. A separate MRF should be filled for each position.

2. Also, to be mentioned if the said additional requirement of manpower is already covered in the budget or not.

3. According to the position required, the appropriate MRF for Permanent / Tenure Based Scaled Contract / Temporary / Outsourced employee should be filled.

4. The proposal would have a brief description of the job to be assigned to the position. It should also include a justification for the creation of the position.

5. The HR Manager will discuss the proposal with the concerned department head and obtain the approval of the Management(Chennai), CEO(AP) for filling up the position.

6. Once the approval is granted, the HR department will initiate the recruitment process.
