Types of employment

Definition of an employee:

Those who are directly employed by the Principal Employer within the premises or outside in connection with the establishment.

 Definition of the Employee

  • Regular
  • Permanent
  • Probationers

Different categories of Company Employee here as under

Regular employee: All employees, who are working for company and engaged directly on company roll, whether on training period, probationer or permanent employee.

Permanent employee: An employee, who is currently working for Company and is engaged directly on company roll and has completed his/her training/probation period and also has been provided employment agreement in writing/confirmation Letter.

Probationer: An employee who is provisionally employed on trial to ascertain his/her suitability to fill a vacancy in a permanent post and has not been confirmed as Permanent in writing by the management. All regular appointments against permanent vacancy shall ordinarily be on probation for a period of 3 (three) months. He / She will be confirmed only after completion of 3 (three) months of probation period. During this period, the employment may be separated after 30 days of notice. The employee may also resign from the services of the company by giving 14 days notice in writing. At the end of the probationary period, we will conduct an evaluation to assess the employee's suitability within the company. Process for confirmation criteria to be evaluated and confirmation email format to employee to be agreed upon  with CEO
