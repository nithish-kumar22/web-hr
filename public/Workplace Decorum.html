Workplace Decorum

IOT Monks Private Limited commitment to these Codes is the cornerstone of our relationship with the employee.

● Individual Responsibility

All Employees are responsible for performing at a level that is consistent with expectations and adhering to the work rules/procedures, and complying with them.

  • Accountability

A senior person cannot pass the buck to his/her juniors for the failure of work or delay. He/She has to have his/her control systems for jobs he/she gets done for his/her juniors and also make them accountable to him/her.

  • Use of office Phone and Mail Systems

  1. Usage of the company telephone for extended long-distance and toll calls for personal purposes is prohibited.

  1. The mail system is reserved for business purposes only. Employees should refrain from sending or receiving personal mail at the workplace. The e-mail system is the property of IOT Monks Private Limited.

● Use of mobile phones

Mobile phones while forming an essential part of day to day communication may hinder the objective quality customer service. Hence it is expected that employees use this judiciously in line with the following guidelines.

  1. All other employees are expected to keep mobile phones in silent mode while interacting with any client or customer or while at customer access  areas.
  2. Unnecessary usage of mobile phones is strictly prohibited in the working hours.

● Honest and ethical conduct

Employees shall be responsible for adherence and compliance with this code of conduct at all times.

  1. Every employee shall be fair and transparent
  2. Employees shall display high standards of personal and professional integrity
  3. Employees shall at all-time act in accordance with high standards of honesty and acceptable ethical conduct.

● Harassment

The Company is committed to providing a work environment free of harassment. Company policy prohibits sexual harassment and harassment based on pregnancy, childbirth, race, religious creed, color, national origin or ancestry, physical or mental disability, medical condition, marital status, age, sexual orientation, or any other basis. All such harassment is unlawful. (Refer POSH policy).

● Substance Abuse

1. Do not work under the influence of alcohol or other substances.

2. Do not possess, use, sell, or transfer illegal drugs

3. Smoking is prohibited throughout the workplace. This policy applies equally to all employees, customers, guests and visitors.

● Health & Safety

1. The safety of people in the workplace is a primary concern of the company. Each of us must comply with all applicable health and safety policies.

2. The company shall strive to provide a safe, healthy and ergonomic working environment for its people. It shall comply with health, safety and environmental Regulations.

3. The company shall be committed to best practice in controlling any hazardous aspect of its business.

4. Employees should consider the potential impact of the activities, products and services of the Company on human health and the environment.

● Workplace Violence

Workplace violence refers to behavior that is personally and objectively offensive, threatening or intimidating. Any person who engages in a threat of violent action on company discretion, will remain off company premises until pending the outcome of an investigation into the incident. Violation of this policy will result in discipline up to and including termination of employment.
