Conflict of Interest

Due to the demands and the competitive nature of the business, we have a special concern with regard to potential conflict of interest that arises out of additional employment. The company expects the employee to devote the full working time and best efforts. The employee should also avoid any situation where the personal interests conflict or appear to conflict with the interest of the company.

● Outside Employment

1. An employee shall not engage in any other business, relationship or activity.

2. Employees are prohibited from engaging in any activity that interferes with their performance or discharge of duties effectively.

● Related parties.

1. Employees should exercise due diligence while transacting with third party Vendors/suppliers/consultants/business Employees, who happen to be related to the Company’s business.

● Selecting Suppliers

1. Our vendors and suppliers contribute significantly in our success and as such must be treated fairly in all transactions. Purchases must be done based on need, quality, price, service, terms and conditions and suppliers must be selected based on quality, price and performance.

2. Suppliers and supplier agreements must be entered into through competitive bids where possible and where not possible must adhere to fair principles.

● Acceptance of Gifts or Favors / kick backs

1. Do not use the position at the company to privately enrich yourself or others.

2. Never ask/accept for a gift or favor from an individual or organization that does business with the company, or is actively seeking to do business with the Company.
