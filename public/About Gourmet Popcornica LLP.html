ABOUT GOURMET POPCORNICA

As with all things, our journey too started with a single wish - to bring to the cinemas of India, the tastiest popcorn in the world. And what better way to do this than to team up with the world's favourite popcorn supplier, Preferred Popcorn, USA! In 2014, our partnership with the renowned farmer grown company in Nebraska, USA, allowed us to import kernels to produce the most delicious popcorn premix for movie-goers across the country. In 2018, with the guidance of our American partners, we began using the 75 year old tradition of nurturing corn, to grow the highest quality corn in our very own home state of Andhra Pradesh.
