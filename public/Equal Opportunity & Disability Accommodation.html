Equal Opportunity & Disability Accommodation

The Company is committed to a policy of equal employment and advancement opportunities for all qualified individuals without regard to race, color, religion, sex, age, marital status, national origin or differently abled (basic job profile). All managers and functional heads are directly responsible for the application of this policy and for ensuring that everyone in their department understands and adheres to this policy.

Disability accommodation

At IOT Monks Private Limited, our commitment to upholding applicable disability laws and fostering equal employment opportunities for qualified individuals with disabilities is unwavering. Our practices reflect this commitment in the following ways:

  • Hiring Practices

Our hiring procedures have been carefully evaluated to provide meaningful employment opportunities to individuals with disabilities. During the pre-employment phase, inquiries are limited to assessing an applicant's ability to carry out the essential functions of the position.

  • Reasonable Accommodation

We ensure that reasonable accommodations are accessible to all disabled Employees, especially if their disability affects their job performance as required by law. Our employment decisions prioritize the merits of each situation and the operational needs of the company, rather than focusing on an individual's disability.

  • Non-Discrimination

IOT Monks Private Limited maintains a strong stance against any form of unlawful discrimination. We do not discriminate against qualified Employees or applicants based on their association with individuals who have disabilities.

  • Comprehensive Commitment

This policy represents the core of our approach, but it's not exhaustive. IOT Monks Private Limited  is dedicated to taking all necessary actions to ensure that individuals with disabilities have equal employment opportunities.
